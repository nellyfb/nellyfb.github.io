Works & Projects

My work ranges from designing original logos for websites to developing full interactive websites. I also design and plan out social media pages to the best it can be for specific audiences. I do services in both Spanish and English language.
I am responsible for what the users and audience interact with in a web application. Implementing visual elements that represent a brand or company and at the same time be understood by the users is vital for growth and expansion on the web.

Process To Making the Breeze Cakes Client Project

This is a project done for my client in web development class. She was 'opening' a cake company and wanted a logo as well a website design that represented her and her brand identity.

Moodboarding

My client had ideas prepared when we met but as a designer I suggested more ideas and colors. Through the creation of this moodboard we found out so much more than we originally intended to. She found out she really likes the blues and purples and didn't want light, soft colors anymore. The final logo was completely inspired by the moodboard and I believe it shows very well.

Logo Creation + Further Explore Brand Identity

My client wanted a logo and website design that represented her and her brand. She wanted a logo that was easy to remember and which people would immediately recognize for being hers. Through moodboarding and creating her logo she also found out more about her brand identity. She no longer wanted be seen as the term she used 'soft' but rather like a 'pop of color' in this grey world.

Sitemapping

Created a sitemap that orgajnizes and explains why and where the webpages are on the website. Through sitemapping I know not to put homepage after every other page

Wireframes & Prototypes

I sketched the skeleton and basic layout of the company's website which is also known as low-fidelity wireframes. After approval of the first wireframes I sketched high-fidelity wireframes. The second design has more detailed out visual attributes and actions that will be on the final page. After the high-fidelity wireframes gets approval we move on to the final touches and fixes which becomes the prototype.
