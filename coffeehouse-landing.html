Works & Projects

My work ranges from designing original logos for websites to developing full interactive websites. I also design and plan out social media pages to the best it can be for specific audiences. I do services in both Spanish and English language.
I am responsible for what the users and audience interact with in a web application. Implementing visual elements that represent a brand or company and at the same time be understood by the users is vital for growth and expansion on the web.

Process To Making the Coffee House Project

This is a real-life example project at web development class of a supposive coffee store chain called "CoffeeHouse International."

Moodboarding

For this project I moodboarded and came up with this soft and almost 'earthy' look to the site and logo. Browns, neutrals, and occasional green was chosen as the color palette. You can see the inspiration and details from the mmodboard on the logo below.

Logo Creation + Further Explore Brand Identity

When coming up with designs for the logo I though of many things including; big and famous chains have a clear and conscise look and brand identity to them. I wanted to have something minimalist but at the same time showcase the 'earthy' look. I wanted the logo to be not-difficult so that it is easy to remember and clear to understand.

Sitemapping

Created a sitemap and list that organizes and explains why and where the webpages are on the website. Through sitemapping I know to not put the homepage after every other page.

Wireframes & Prototypes

I sketched the skeleton and basic layout of the company's website which is also known as low-fidelity wireframes. After approval of the first wireframes I sketched high-fidelity wireframes. The second design has more detailed out visual attributes and actions that will be on the final page. After the high-fidelity wireframes gets approval we move on to the final touches and fixes which becomes the prototype.
